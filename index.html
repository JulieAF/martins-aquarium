Tank Care Tips
1. Acclimate your fish to the water.
2. Float fish in their bag.
3. Maintain pH balance and other chemical levels.
4. Make sure water temperature is right.
5. Change water regularly.
6. Clean tank glass and other structures.
7. Choose the right size for your tank.
8. Pick compatible fish species.
